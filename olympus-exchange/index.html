GAME RULES
✕
How to Play

• Watch the price chart and the broker's signal

• Decide whether the price will go UP (LONG) or DOWN (SHORT)

• You have 10 seconds to make your decision each round

• Correct prediction = +points and keep your life

• Wrong prediction = lose 1 life

Lives & Game Over

• You start with 3 lives

• Lose all lives = Game Over

• Your final score is submitted to the leaderboard

The Brokers

ZEUS — Powerful but sometimes overconfident. Strong early game.

MEDUSA — Cold and calculating. Very precise but unpredictable.

MINOTAUR — Wild and dangerous. Big rewards when correct, but can take 2 lives if wrong.

Scoring

• The faster and more accurately you predict, the more points you earn.

• Try to survive as many rounds as possible.
